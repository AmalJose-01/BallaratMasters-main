About the Ballarat Masters Badminton Club

The Ballarat Masters Badminton Club is a welcoming and inclusive community for adult players of all skill levels who are passionate about badminton. Based in Ballarat, we promote fun, fitness, and friendship through regular social games, competitions, and events. Whether you're returning to the sport or picking up a racket for the first time, you'll find a supportive environment to improve your skills and enjoy the game
